How a project runs

Same four beats whether it's a 30-second reel or a full EP master.

STEP 01

Intake

You send the brief and raw files. I confirm scope, turnaround, and price before anything starts.

STEP 02

First pass

A draft edit, layout, or rough mix comes back with notes on the choices I made.

STEP 03

Revisions

You mark up what to change. Two rounds are included in every quote by default.

STEP 04

Delivery

Final files land in whatever format and spec your platform needs, plus source files on request.

Why one person, three crafts

Most projects touch more than one medium anyway—a video needs a thumbnail, a mix needs cover art, a brand needs its logo animated. Working across all three means nothing gets lost explaining context to a second or third freelancer.

I've spent the last few years splitting time between an NLE, a design canvas, and a DAW, freelancing for creators, small labels, and independent brands who need one dependable hand across their whole output rather than three separate ones.
